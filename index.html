Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have this particular base size?

We have all kinds of base sizes for many different games. We typically only sell round and oval bases with a flat bevel, but we can make much more. If you ask us for a particular size we can usually make it, but some requests take longer to fulfill depending on how complex or unique the order is.

How long will it take to get my order?

Standard USPS domestic shipping times and rates apply. Sometimes custom orders take longer to fulfill but you should hear from us pretty quickly after you order.

What are your bases made of?

When we design a base it is typically hand-sculpted either in clay or digitally. We then cast it in resin. We do this to keep the costs as low as possible so that we can pass savings onto you the customer. Some of our products are 3D Printed, but our bases are not.
